## 〔 01 〕 About me

I didn't get into data science because I had a grand plan. I started with **curiosity.**

One dataset led to another, one notebook became many more, and over time that journey grew into machine learning projects, computer vision models, NLP experiments, Kaggle competitions, and AI hackathons.

I'm **Saman Fatima** — a data scientist from Pakistan 🇵🇰 and a Kaggle Grandmaster (rank **#191 of 62,137**, highest ever **#24**).

I work across exploratory data analysis, classical ML, deep learning for vision and language, time-series forecasting, and more recently LLM and agent-based projects through hackathons.

> *Start with the data. Ask honest questions. Learn something useful. Share it clearly.*

I write notebooks the way I wish they had been written when I was learning — with reasoning, context, and reproducible results. Outside of building, I help aspiring Kagglers find their footing on the platform.
