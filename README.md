<h3>01 — About me</h3>

I didn't get into data science because I had a grand plan.

I started with curiosity.

One dataset led to another, one notebook became many more, and over time that journey grew into machine learning projects, computer vision models, NLP experiments, Kaggle competitions, and AI hackathons.

I'm **Saman Fatima**, a data scientist from Pakistan and a Kaggle Grandmaster (rank **#191 of 62,137**, highest rank **#24**).

I work across exploratory data analysis, classical machine learning, deep learning for vision and language, time-series forecasting, and more recently LLM and agent-based projects through hackathons.

Most of the work you'll find here comes from learning, experimenting, competing, and documenting what I discover along the way.

My approach is simple:

> Start with the data.  
> Ask honest questions.  
> Learn something useful.  
> Share it clearly.

I try to write notebooks the way I wish they had been written when I was learning — with reasoning, context, and reproducible results rather than just final answers.

Outside of building projects, I enjoy helping aspiring Kagglers and data-science learners find their footing on the platform.
